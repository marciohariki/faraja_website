M-PESA

You can also donate to FARAJA via MPESA on your Safaricom mobile phone number.

Go to the Safaricom Menu on your phone,

  • select the MPESA option,
  • select the Pay Bill option.
  • enter FARAJA's Business number 509700,
  • for the account number enter the FARAJA business number 509700 again
  • enter a donation amount of your choice
  • put in your PIN
  • confirm your transaction
  • wait for a confirmation message from MPESA

With these simple steps, you too can help FARAJA give HELP, HOPE, LIFE.

For any enquires on the MPESA donation or any other donation process, kindly send an email to info@farajacancersupport.org or call us on +254 (0)727 529 287

If you have any questions or queries about the M-PESA service go to the M-PESA homepage on the Safaricom website.
